Kuta Beach

We highly recommend you to visit Kuta Beach. Kuta is a tourist area, administratively an urban village and the capital of Kuta District, Badung Regency, southern Bali, Indonesia. it was one of the first towns on Bali to see substantial tourist development, and as a beach resort remains one of Indonesia's major tourist destinations.

Other Places

Lovina Beach

Maybe for most tourists who have visited Bali, the name of Lovina Beach is still very strange to be heard. Lovina Beach is one of the beaches in the North Bali region, precisely close to Singaraja City. Here you can see a collection of dolphins swimming while jumping up and down because Lovina beach is indeed the natural habitat of the dolphins.

Uluwatu Beach

Uluwatu Beach is the next tour that you must visit while on the island of Bali. Although the location of this beach is quite hidden, but the natural beauty that is in it is like a hidden spike. The beach, also known by the other name Blue Point, requires visitors to descend a number of narrow stairs to get to Uluwatu Beach.

Bekasih Temple

The biggest Hindu temple in Besakih Village, Kec. Rendang, Kab. Karangasem, the highest temple stands at the foot of Mount Agung, which is the highest mountain on the island of Bali. In the Besakih Temple complex there are 1 Basukian temple and 17 other temples. Can be given, if the Besakih Temple is the center of activity of all the temples on the island of Bali.
